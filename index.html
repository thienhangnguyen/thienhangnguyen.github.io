About me

I am a Member of Technical Staff (Research) at Contextual AI, working on agentic search and information retrieval.

I completed my Ph.D. in March 2025 at Khoury College of Computer Sciences at Northeastern University, where I started the Fall of 2020 and was very fortunate to be advised by Huy Le Nguyen. There, I worked on optimization and machine learning, which is the topic of my Thesis.

Before Khoury, I graduated from University of California, Irvine in 2020 with a B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering and a B.S. in Mathematics. In the Summer of 2019, I worked with Andrea Bertozzi at UCLA's REU program in Computational and Applied Mathematics.
